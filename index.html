Macintosh Student Developers for OS X
MS-DOS X is a student-taught class about programming for Mac and iOS. This semester (Fall 2014), we're meeting on Mondays and Wednesdays from 8pm to 10pm in 200 Sutardja Dai Hall (the Mac lab - "Apple Orchard" - in the CITRIS building).

Announcements

  • If you still aren't able to register, email one of us your (TeleBears) name, SID, and whether you want 98 or 198 units (class is the same).
  • If you still don't have Card-key access, email one of us your TeleBears name and SID.
  • Lab bulletin: If Xcode gives you "out of space" errors, open Terminal.app and run ~msdosx/cleanup.sh. If the iOS Simulator doesn't work, run ~msdosx/fix-sim.sh (you'll have to run it every time you use a different lab computer).
  • Additional account forms are available in the lab. You can get one after an evening's lecture if you don't already have one.
  • "Office hours" are lab times.
  • Final project presentations: 2014.05.09 10am-12pm in Sutardja Dai Hall, Room 200 (Apple Orchard lab)
